My God Will Do Exceedingly

Isaiah 41:17 and 18When the poor and needy seek water, and there is none, and their tongue faileth for thirst, I the LORD will hear them, I the God of Israel will not forsake them. I will open rivers in high places, and fountains in the midst of the valleys: I will make the wilderness a pool of water, and the dry land springs of water. Our Lord is Jehovah Jire. He is a great provider and will supply exceedingly above what we asked for. According to the book of Prophet Isaiah a man that is in need water to drink and kill his taste is offered a river. That same God that spoke with prophet Isaiah is rising against your request today, He has done it before and will do it again. Are you looking unto Him for the fruit of the womb? The Lord of Abraham is still alive and will answer you with great testimony. Here this: Genesis 16:10 And the angel of the LORD said unto her, I will multiply thy seed exceedingly, that it shall not be numbered for multitude. He is God whatever he promises He will do. Though it may tarry but it will come. Apostle Paul reiterate in the book of Ephesians 3:20: Now unto him that is able to do exceeding abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that worketh in us. What other assurance do you need to know that we serve a miracle working God. If you diligently hearken to Him and walk according to His will, you will surely eat the fruits of the land. The Lord of Solomon is laying an emphasis 1Kings 3:13 And I have also given thee that which thou hast not asked, both riches, and honour: so that there shall not be any among the kings like unto thee all thy days. The Lord knows you and He is interested in you as a project. Our Lord will never have an abandoned project or fail in executing His project. You are one and He will see it to the end. Philippians 1:6 Being confident of this very thing, that he which hath begun a good work in you will perform it until the day of Jesus Christ. Be of good cheer
